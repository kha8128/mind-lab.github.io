# Outreach & Mentoring

Our group is committed to mentoring students and broadening participation in AI-enabled scientific research, with an emphasis on materials, energy, environmental, and engineering applications.

## AI for the Public Good

Our group participates in Binghamton University's AI for the Public Good initiative by mentoring undergraduate students on applications of artificial intelligence and data science to scientific and engineering challenges.

## Future Activities

We plan to continue expanding undergraduate research mentoring, cross-institutional summer research opportunities, and public-facing educational activities related to artificial intelligence for scientific discovery and engineering.
